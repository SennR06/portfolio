DESIGN PROCESS

I design in an intuitive way. I rarely start from individual screens, but from the journey someone takes and what that experience should feel like. I like to map how a user arrives, which steps they go through and where they might need a small nudge. From there I make choices in flows, states and copy that feel calm and logical for the user, even when the concept is playful or surprising. I work in a focused and structured way, often turning a loose collection of ideas or wireframes into one clear story that makes sense from the first click to the final interaction. I am curious and driven, always looking for ways to improve my skills and to make work that has real impact instead of delivering something that feels half finished.
